Tournament Rules

· You must be 18 years or older.

· Professional PUBG players are not allowed to participate.

· No alternate or smurf accounts. Please play on your main account. We will check accounts for age, games played, etc.

· Your position in the queue is locked in once you are signed up. It is first come, first serve basis. If all spots have been filled, then you will be put on a wait list. If for some reason you or your team cannot make it, please let myself or Froddoyo know as soon as possible.

· Cheating at any level is not tolerated. That includes stream sniping. If you are caught cheating, you will be banned immediately from the tournament, from all platforms, and your team's prize money (if applicable) will be given to charity.

· Only teams of two players are allowed to participate. Substitutions will be only be allowed if given a 48 hour notice from the start of the tourney. If you cannot find a substitute, then you will be moved to the bottom of the wait list and the next team will take your spot.

· Toxic behavior will NOT be tolerated. No second chances. You will be kicked on the first offense.

· There will be a 5 minute intermission between each game to give participants time to load into the next lobby. If you are having issues connecting, please notify myself or Froddoyo as soon as possible. We may extend the break a few minutes but unfortunately we cannot keep everybody waiting if your team cannot make it into the lobby.

· Upon signing up, you will be given a team number. This number corresponds to the voice channel you are required to use in the TOURNAMENT CHAT channel in the Discord. You are also required to use the corresponding team slot in the tournament lobby.

· The password between each round will be located in the PASSWORDS channel in Discord. Do NOT share this password with anybody who is not participating in the tournament.

· You are free to stream your perspective of the tourney without delay.

· The tourney will be casted on both of our Twitch channels. You are more than welcome to join the stream between matches but we ask that you minimize the stream when matches begin.

Please re-read the rules before the start of the Tournament!

There may be changes or revisions!
