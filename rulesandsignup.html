Tournament Rules

· Eligibility: Participants must be 18 years of age or older.

· Professional Players: Professional PUBG players are not allowed to participate.

· Account Restrictions: No alternate or smurf accounts are allowed. Players must use their main account, and accounts will be checked for age, games played, and other criteria.

· Registration: Spots are limited and filled on a first-come, first-served basis. Once registered, a team's position in the queue is locked in. If for some reason a team cannot make it, they must inform the organizers as soon as possible. Waitlisted teams will take the spot of absent teams.

· Cheating: Cheating in any form is not tolerated, including stream sniping. Anyone caught cheating will be immediately banned from the tournament and from all platforms. Their team's prize money, if applicable, will be given to charity.

· Teams: Only teams of two players are allowed to participate. Substitutions will only be allowed up to 48-hour notice from the start of the tournament. If a substitute cannot be found, the team will be moved to the bottom of the waitlist and the next team will take their spot.

· Behavior: Toxic behavior will not be tolerated, and offenders will be kicked on the first offense.

· Intermission: There will be a 5-minute intermission between each game to allow participants time to load into the next lobby. If a team cannot connect, they must inform the organizers as soon as possible. The intermission may be extended by a few minutes, but the tournament cannot be held up for too long.

· Communication: Upon signing up, teams will be assigned a team number, which corresponds to the voice channel they are required to use in the TOURNAMENT CHAT channel in the Discord. They are also required to use the corresponding team slot in the tournament lobby

· Passwords: The password for each round will be located in the PASSWORDS channel in Discord. Participants are not to share this password with anyone not participating in the tournament.Communication: Upon signing up, teams will be assigned a team number, which corresponds to the voice channel they are required to use in the TOURNAMENT CHAT channel in the Discord. · Streaming: Participants are free to stream their perspective of the tournament without delay.

· The tourney will be casted on both of our Twitch channels. You are more than welcome to join the stream between matches but we ask that you minimize the stream when matches begin.

Please re-read the rules before the start of the Tournament!

There may be changes or revisions!
